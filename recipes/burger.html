Description

The Chicken Sandwich with Gravy is the ultimate comfort food, combining juicy, seasoned chicken with the rich, velvety smoothness of savory gravy. Whether the chicken is pan-fried for a crispy crust or grilled for a leaner version, it serves as the hearty centerpiece of this satisfying sandwich. Nestled inside a soft, toasted bun, the chicken is generously smothered with warm gravy that soaks slightly into the bread, creating a melt-in-your-mouth texture with every bite.

This sandwich is both rustic and indulgent, often served with a side of mashed potatoes, coleslaw, or fries for a filling meal. The gravy adds depth and warmth, making it ideal for cozy dinners or rainy-day lunches. Optional toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, or pickles can add a touch of freshness and crunch, but the star remains the tender chicken paired with the flavorful, comforting gravy that ties it all together.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (optional for a crispy version)
  • 2 sandwich rolls or buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter or oil for frying
  • 1 cup chicken gravy (homemade or store-bought)
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato slices, pickles

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge the chicken in flour, dip in beaten egg, and coat with breadcrumbs if using.
  3. Heat butter or oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden and fully cooked (internal temp 165°F / 74°C).
  4. Warm the gravy in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  5. Toast the sandwich rolls lightly for extra texture.
  6. Place the cooked chicken on the rolls and pour warm gravy over the top.
  7. Add optional toppings if desired and serve immediately.
